Recommendation in short

Now (web, $0): ship Variant 2 (gold on dark) for the most expensive first impression, or Variant 1 (SF-Symbols style) for the safe Apple-grade default — both monochrome, both the direct opposite of cartoon 3D.

The brand move (~$400–800, 1–2 weeks): commission a bespoke 12-icon line set via a Dribbble designer, briefed to match Phosphor Thin geometry — a unique icon language is what makes the app feel truly Aman/Aesop-tier.

Native iOS later: use real SF Symbols 8 (free, Apple-only). Full options table, paid sets & prices, and the Apple/SF-Symbols explanation are in RECOMMENDATION.md.
